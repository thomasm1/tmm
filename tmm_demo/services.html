Better Statistical Consulting provides many services, for professionals, students, and researchers alike. Danielle has experience analyzing data from diverse fields, including economics, environmental conservation, education, mental health, sociology, and business, among others. BSC offers the main services:

  • Data Analysis. From the most basic descriptive statistics to more complex analyses, BSC can clean data, run your analyses, and produce clear, concise reports explaining the results. Danielle's personal attention ensures that you will understand every analysis.
  • Research Design. Before beginning an experiment or survey, let BSC work with you to ensure validity and reliability. From sampling to questionnaire design to implementation, Danielle will guide your survey from beginning to end.
  • Data Management. Data needs to be stored after collection; let Danielle's experience with Access, Survey Monkey, and other online databases smooth the process. Surveys and forms are crisp and professional.
  • Data Analysis Results Interpretation. BSC will provide a second look at analyses performed elsewhere or simply review a pending report for accuracy. Contact Danielle for more information on results interpretation.
  • Tutoring. BSC provides statistics tutoring limited to the Albuquerque metro area. Classes are by the hour, on a one-time basis or ongoing according to need. Undergraduate, graduate, and high school students are welcome for tutoring. Email for rates and policies.

All services come with professional, personal, and fast service.
