We are now recognized as a non-profit org. in NM and are now filing our 501(c)(3) !!

GGA has been asked to have a booth at the Earth Day Event, Sunday, April 27! We would love to have people come out and support us!

 

Our Mission:

To support and inspire others anonymously one gift at a time with acts of kindness and to continue this compassion chain, one person, one community at a time, through collaborations with nonprofits in the Albuquerque area.

 

 

 

The 2013 Christmas gifting project is complete but our year round gifting still continues. We need your help year round. We are always seeking volunteers to assist us with pickup and sorting of donations. Also, please keep us in mind throughout the year with donations of household items and furniture. With your help we can continue to donate to the organizations helping those in need!

Check our facebook page regularly for dates of our pickups or email us your contact information if you are interested in volunteering. Don't forget to like us on facebook!

Join us in spreading cheer and making the world a better place one random act of kindness at a time!
