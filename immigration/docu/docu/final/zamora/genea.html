Loic The Louic Family name, heralding the 19th century, is currently being researched. For more information contact the webmaster.
Zamora The Zamora Family also owned La Placita restaurant, which is across the street East of Zamora Mansion.
Chavez The Chavez Family, two generations ago, was proud to represent the first United States Senator: Dennis Chavez. Today, the Chavez name bears the name of the four-term New Mexico State Senator: Tito D. Chavez.
Maestas The Maestas Family is first derived from Eileen Chavez-Maestas, blessed be her name, whose stewardship is passed to her three sons, Victor, Michael, and Thomas.
