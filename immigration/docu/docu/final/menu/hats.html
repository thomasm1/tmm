The Old Town Hat Shop and Accessory Boutique is located in the historic Old Town section of Albuquerque, New Mexico. We offer a wide selection of hats for every occasion, from the classic western look to beautiful handcrafted creations.

All of our hats are constructed of the highest quality materials available and carefully sized to fit your measurements perfectly, ensuring you comfort and style at a surprisingly affordable price. We also offer a wide range of accessories from handbags to belts to complete your look.
