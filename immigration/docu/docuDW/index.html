Plaza Primorosa situates itself in the southeast corner of the Zamora's Historical Mansion. Zamora mansion comprises the north facing side of Old Town Plaza. Plaza Primorosa offers New Mexico souvenirs, including Mexican imports, religious and artistic carvings, and Santa Fe Incense.


  • Oaxacan sculptures
  • Silver Crosses
  • Woodcarved Santos
  • Leather Purses and Hats
  • Knives
