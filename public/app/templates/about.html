Hello, I'm Thomas, Thank you for visiting my site.

Technology shapes much of my life, and in many respects it frames my future plans!
Technology is integral to my professional, academic, and enterprising endeavors, and I love to chime in about so many topics of the Day...

My career pursuits has focused on data analytsis on topics like education evaluation in K-12 and higher education. Now, extend my data-related experience into web-app designs and development, specializing in data visualization with JavaScript and Python programming. In 2015, I earned my Masters in Quantitative Sociology from the University of New Mexico--an education that continues to guide my professional aims. I love to read and study languages, so naturally I earned my Bachelors in French History. I began sociology of education doctoral studies at l'Université de Montréal in the Fall of 2016, but discontinued in order to pursue my tech career.

My social media, you can find me ... Quora, Historium, Instagram, Facebook, Meet-Up, Hiking Group, and Alliance Francaise. I have lived in Paris, France and New York and Texas. I currently live in the Wild West, Albuquerque, New Mexico where I contract my small business, T M M, providing Contractual Services and Consulting for Big & Small Data and Web solutions since 2016
