1. A player rolls the die and collects current points until rolling snake-eyes or even one '1', at which point the player loses all his/her current points. 2. A player avoids losing all his points by 'holding' at some point, thus keeping the collected points.
3. The players therefore alternate turns at dice rolls, until the first Player reaches a hundred (100) points, which is the victor.
