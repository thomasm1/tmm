Gifting Stories and Testimonies

“I think it is great how you are providing an opportunity for people to recycle what they no longer wish to keep and give it to a good cause.  Plus, I love knowing that someone will have the delight of opening a surprise gift for the holidays that he/she otherwise would not have.  Your project makes me feel a part of something bigger than me, which is very gratifying” 
Laura Maria Hornsby

 I helped distribute unsolicited gifts to random people with Kalei in December of 2012. It was a memorable experience. The others had wrapped and bagged gifts and filled stockings for several days. They had designated some as best for kids, men or women so we tried to select an appropriate gift for whomever we stopped. People were so surprised to have something offered to them. I think some people may have thought we were strange but then amazed when they realized that this was a random act of kindness/generosity and that all we wanted was for them to pass it on as possible.
Wanda

“It is so much fun to watch the ripple effect of this and to see total strangers tear up with joy from receiving a gift from a stranger who wants nothing from them but who simply wants to spread some positive energy in the community”

Kalei

"Up until recently, Random Acts of Kindness were something I only appreciated on bumper stickers. When Kalei approached me to participate in her gift givers project last winter, I jumped on the occasion. Initially many people were cautious when we approached them as total strangers, only to light up like Christmas trees when they realized we were gifting them. A few months later I was in a retail store when a total stranger approached me and several others with store-wide savings coupons. She informed me she always prints extra coupons when she shops and hands them out. Both of these experiences lifted my spirit and give me hope that a brighter future lies ahead and begins with each and every one of us." 

Stephen P. Weiss, M.D.

I cannot express to you the joy that being a part of this brings. From the collecting, bagging, to giving the joy within you builds. When you see a stranger’s face go from confusion, then slowly to understanding. That's when their smile appears,changing everything. At that moment pure joy has taken over. It is amazing! In a world that teaches us it's all about self. It is a blessing for my girls to be involved with the sole purpose of others. Here is the best part. It doesn't matter what your skin is, what your job or income is, or how different your life's might be. This is a heart thing. Hearts working side by side….Why? Here is the kicker. Just because. Giving what every one of us desires. Simple kindness, love and joy. There is nothing like it! __JENNA

"When I first joined Gift Givers Anonymous and helped distribute gifts to unsuspecting receivers I was a bit apprehensive about approaching strangers.  I thought, “If anyone approached me in such a way, I would probably take a step back or keep walking.”  After watching Kalei approach people with a big smile, kind and comforting words, I thought, “I can do this.”  So I did.  The more I handed gifts the more comfortable I became.  The more comfortable I became the more I watched the people I approached relax.
Watching people’s reactions when we handed them a gift for no reason, other than to spread some gifts of Kindness was pure JOY!  The excitement on the people’s faces ... [click to read more about Mary Jo's experience]

I was fascinated with the project when you first told me about it. I think it is awesome to see people give to those in need without expecting anything in return. 
It is great to see how just a little gift and thought can really make someone else’s day and make their face glow! It has been a good experience to get my other friends involved in as well.
Giving from the heart is the most rewarding feeling. We are all so blessed and it is great to have an opportunity to share that blessing with others as well. 
(This project) has grown like an epidemic and epidemic of giving!! 
-Emily Arzate
