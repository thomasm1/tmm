Donations are needed of new or gently used household goods to help families in need. These families are being served by small non profit organizations who help house the homeless or assist those in housing crisis. Most of these organizations have little to no storage and are always seeking household goods for the families they are serving. Any items we receive are gifted directly to these families being served by such organizations. Items that are always in need include but are not limited to:

  • Durable Medical Equipment
  • Art & Craft Supplies
  • Computer Equipment
  • "Found" Art Supplies (cloth, thread, bottle caps, shoe boxes, shells etc.)
  • School Supplies (notebook paper, calculators, notebooks)
  • Towels (bath towels and kitchen towels)
  • Silverware (LOTS of need for this item )
  • Pots/Pans (LOTS of need for this item)
  • Dishes, cups, plates
  • Trashcans
  • Shower liners
  • Household furniture such as couches and dining room tables
  • Mattresses and boxsprings
What is the Christmas Gift Giving project? 

We collect donated gently used items or even new items you may no longer want.  Then at Christmas time volunteers gather together to wrap gifts. On one special day we all go out into the community and give gifts away to people!! 

We would love to have this project expand even more and incorporate all ages (last Christmas we had ages 10 - 82 involved!) into all aspects of the program from gift packaging day to gift distribution.  Even people who would love to bake Christmas cookies that we can take to a retirement center as a surprise gift!  So many possibilities!  

PLEASE DONATE "gently used" items to be used to give out to people “randomly” at Christmas time.
These are anonymous acts of kindness that touch the receivers in amazing ways.
We need items donated anytime of the year. (Email Kalei and she will pick up the items.)

Examples of items that can be gifted are:
Decorative items, picture frames, purses, wallets, scarves, jewelry, coffee mugs, stuffed animals, nice magnets, jewelry boxes, body wash, nice Christmas ornaments, gift cards, nice soaps or bath salts, and the list continues.
We especially need items men would appreciate.  Also, tons of gift bags and tissue paper for this year's project.  If you have some used gift bags and tissue paper we could use them!
