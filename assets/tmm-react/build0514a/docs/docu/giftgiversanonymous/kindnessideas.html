    Some simple suggestions on ways to spread some acts of kindness in your community ...

    If you think you can't make a difference...ask yourself, is that really true? With the first brick, builds a foundation...with the first step, starts a journey...with the first thought, begins a Dream that has changed the world...out of small things, BIG things can be accomplished, so...start with yourself, BE THE CHANGE YOU WISH TO SEE IN THIS WORLD!                                                             --Joi Holloway

  • Help someone with a chore unexpectedly
  • Write a thank you note to someone who inspired you
  • Pay for the person behind you in line
  • Leave a snack for an unsuspecting co-worker or friend
  • Open the door for someone
  • Leave the exact change for the person who comes after you at the laundrymat
  • Smile at a stranger while looking them straight in the eyes
  • Keep some non-perishable snacks in the car to give to a homeless person on the street corner
  • Make a conscious effort to compliment someone you don’t know
  • Gift a stranger a $5 gift card for a coffeehouse or retail store. Enclose a “Smile Card” in the envelope (for more information on Smile Cards go to www.kindspring.org)
    •

    For more suggestions on ways to pay it forward and perform small acts of kindness go to www.kindspring.org.
